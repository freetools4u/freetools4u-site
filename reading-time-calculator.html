Estimate How Long It Takes to Read

Use this free tool to calculate how long your audience might take to read your article, essay, or blog post. Based on average reading speed.

How It Works

  • Paste any text into the box or enter total word count
  • Click "Calculate Reading Time"
  • The tool estimates how long it would take to read the content at ~225 words per minute (WPM)

Why Use This Tool?

  • Plan better blog structure based on estimated reader attention
  • Optimize articles for engagement and time on page
  • Inform readers before they start reading
  • Fast, free, mobile-optimized, and works in-browser

FAQs

Is it accurate?

It’s based on an average reading speed of 225 WPM. Individual results may vary, but it's a reliable general estimate.

Can I use this for school assignments or reports?

Yes! Students and teachers use it all the time to estimate reading times for essays, speeches, or articles.
